Hate Mask?

﻿﻿Difficulty in breathing? Fog up glasses? Ruin makeup?

Wearing masks for an extended period, especially outside in the heat, are causing people to have difficulty breathing, fog up glasses and ruin makeup.   

We been through the pain, so we decided to design this simple yet effective all-in-one solution - Omieo™ Mask Frame.

Omieo™ MaskFrame accompanies thousands of our customers in the service industry, nurses, teachers, construction workers and other everyday Americans to make their daily lives much more comfortable. 

Omieo™ Mask Frame creates more breathing room between your mouth and the mask. You will feel much cooler and breathe much easier with a face-mask on.

"I am a teacher and have to wear a mask all day. I struggle with wearing a mask because I have asthma, and I feel like I can't breathe with the material touching my nose and lips. Features:

  • More Space for Breathing - holds up the mask fabric around the mouth to create more breathing space when wearing a mask
  • Makeup Saver - creates space between your mouth and your lips protecting your lipstick from being ruined. 
  • Prevents Mask Acne - Stopping the mask from constantly touching your face prevents acne caused by masks
  • Safe & Reusable - Made from food-grade soft silicone, making it safe, washable, and reusable. The support bracket does not cause rashes, even on sensitive skin.

How Does It Work?

Simply clips Omieo™ Mask Frame onto the inside of your preferred mask and creates a small amount of space in between your face and the mask's material. This allows you to speak clearly, breathe easier, and keeps your face cleaner, while still protecting the people around you from your breath.

Can I Re-use It?

YES! And please do! Omieo™ Mask Frame is made out of medical-grade PE (upgraded from silicon to remove any smells), and is made to be worn, washed, and reused. All you have to do to clean it off is use your preferred soap, give it a rinse, and dry it back off! Ready to go again for another day of school, work, or out and about!
