Shipping

Yes, we ship all over the world. Shipping costs will apply, and will be added at checkout. We run discounts and promotions all year, so stay tuned for exclusive deals.

It depends on where you are. Orders will be processed in 1 to 2 business days before dispatching it to our courier provider.

The delivery itself will usually take 1 to 2 weeks for US and other major countries (i.e., Canada, UK, Australia and New Zealand).  Other countries might take 3 to 4 weeks.

Delivery details will be provided in your confirmation email.

IMPORTANT: Covid-19 pandemic has impacted some of our delivery, however we are doing everything we can including taking up private channels to ensure timely delivers. 

We use all major carriers, and local courier partners, and we will provide you with a tracking number to tracking your delivery.

Return

We always aim for make sure our customers love our products, but if you do need to return an order, we’re happy to help. Just email us directly and we’ll take you through the process.

*Customer will have to cover their own shipping cost.

**For damaged goods, please contact support with a picture of the damage to get a replacement.
